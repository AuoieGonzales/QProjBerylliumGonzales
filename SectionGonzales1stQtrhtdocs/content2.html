Jin

Birth Name: Kim Seokjin

Jin of BTS was born on December 4, 1992 and is the eldest member of the group. His hometown is at Gyeonggi-do Province, Gwacheon, South Korea. In 2010, Kim Seokjin was casted by a Big Hit staff after getting off the bus because of his looks. He didn’t have any experience in singing and dancing, but he trained and became the 4th confirmed member of BTS. He debuted as the vocalist and visual of BTS. He graduated with a degree in art and acting at Konkuk University.

Jin is a songwriter and producer and helped in making a few songs from the group. Aside from this, he also released his own solo songs and k-drama sound tracks such as “Abyss”, “Tonight”, “Yours”, and “Super Tuna”. Jin sung “It’s Definitely You”, the OST of “Hwarang: Poet Warrior Youth” with his member V who was an actor from the drama. “Tonight” and “Abyss” were unofficial songs by Jin on the group’s SoundCloud account. “Tonight” was written by him with 2 other songwritters and his member RM. It was released on 2019 as part of their Festa: the yearly event celebrating the group’s anniversary. He wrote this song for his pets that passed away - sugar gliders and his childhood dog. “Abbys” was released the day before his birthday in 2020. Jin wrote this song with the other writers and RM. Jin’s song “Yours” is the OST of the 2021 K-drama “Jirisan”. On December 4, 2021, Jin’s birthday, he released “Super Tuna” on the channel BAGNTANTV on youtube with a performance video. He produced and wrote this song with BUMZU, a singer, songwriter, and producer under Pledis Entertainment - a company under the same label as BTS, Hybe Labels.


Jimin

Birth Name: Park Jimin

Born on the 13th of October 1995, Park Jimin is the lead vocalist and main dancer of BTS. He is part of the group’s dance line. His birth place is in Busan, South Korea where he studied contemporary dance at the Busan High School of Arts. One of Jimin’s teacher from his High School encouraged him to audition to be an idol. He chose to audition at Big Hit Entertainment and became a trainee of the company in 2012. He is the final and 7th member to join the line up for BTS.

The BTS member also takes part in writing and producing some of the group’s song. Jimin wrote his songs “Christmas Love” and “Promise” together with the help of his member RM and a Big Hit producer, Slow Rabbit. Jimin also produced these 2 songs together with the producer. “Promise” was an unofficial release by Jimin on SoundCloud in 2018. On the day before the Christmas of 2020, “Christmas Love” was released on Youtube as gift for ARMYs.


V

Birth Name: Kim Taehyung

Kim Taehyung was born on December 30, 1995 in Busan, South Korea and grew up in Geochang County, Gyeongsang, South Korea. He came from a family of farmers and he was raised by his grandmother. Taehyung joined Big Hit Music in 2011 and is the 6th member to be confirmed as part of BTS. An audition was taking place at Daegu at that time and he went there just to support his friend, but the staff encouraged him to audition too. He was hesitant at first and called his father to ask for permission. He auditioned and was the only person in his hometown to become a trainee at Big Hit. V made his debut as one of BTS’s vocalists and became part of the dance line after the release of his solo song “Singularity” from the group’s album, “Love Yourself: Tear”.

This member doesn’t only know how to sing and dance, he also participated in writing and producing some of the songs for the group and he even acts. Taehyung is part of the “Wooga Squad”, the name of their friend group. It consists of Taehyung, Choi Woo-shik, Park Seo-joon, Park Hyung-sik, and Peakboy. He made his acting debut as Kim Taehyung at the 2016 K-drama, “Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth” portraying the role of Seok Han-sung together with his friends Park Seo-joon and Park Hyung-sik.Taehyung, together with one of his members Jin, sang the OST for the drama. V also made a cameo in the music video “Gyopo Hairstyle” of Peakboy. His solo songs outside of BTS are “Scenery”, “Snow Flower”, “Winter Bear”, “Sweet Night”, and “Christmas Tree”. “Scenery” (written fully by V) and “Winter Bear” (V participated in writing this song) are unoffcial songs released by V. “Snow Flower ft. Peakboy” was released on his 25th birthday (2020). He wrote this song and produced it with Peakboy. “Sweet Night” and “Christmas Tree” are both OSTs. “Sweet Night” (2020) is written and produced by V with the help of other producers and composers. It’s the OST for the K-drama “Itaewon Class” where his friend, Park Seo-joon, portrays the male protagonist of the story. “Christmas Tree” is the OST of the 2021 K-drama “Our Beloved Summer” starring his friend Choi Woo-shik as the male main lead.


Jungkook

Birth Name: Jeon Jungkook

The youngest member of the group, Jeon Jungkook, was born in Busan, South Korea. His birthday is in September 1, 1997. His journey started when he auditioned for a competition show, but he didn’t get accepted. After this, he got casted by 7 different entertainment companies including big ones, one of which is a small company named Big Hit Entertainment. The reason why he chose to join Big Hit, a relatively smaller company, is because he saw the rap performance of the member RM and thought that it’s cool. In 2011, Jungkook officially became a trainee and is the 5th member to be part of the line up. To improve his dancing skils, emotion, and stage presence, he was sent to Los Angeles to train.

Jungkook debuted as the center, main vocalist, lead dancer and sub-rapper of the group. He’s part of the dance line. Jungkook’s solo songs that’s not part of the album of BTS are “Still With You” and “Decalcomania”. Both song were unofficial release. He compossed and produced “Still With You” with the Big Hit producer Pdogg and wrote “Decalcomania”. “Still With You” was released in 2020 as part of their Festa: the yearly event celebrating the group’s anniversary. Jungkook uploaded “Decalcomania” on twitter for his birthday in 2019. He also wrote the Japanese OST “Your Eyes Tell” for the drama of the same title. This was sung by the whole group and is part of their Japanese album, Map of the Soul: 7 ~ The Journey ~.
