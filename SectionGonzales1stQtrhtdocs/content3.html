RM

Birth Name: Kim Namjoon

Kim Namjoon was born in Ilsan, South Korea on September 12, 1994. RM’s parents were against his dream of pursuing music at first. He used to be an underground rapper by the name Runch Randa before auditioning to Big Hit. He was the 1st member of the group and joined the company in 2010. He debuted with the stage name Rap Monster, but later changed it to RM in 2017. RM stands for Real Me. RM is the main rapper and leader of BTS. Ever since the start, RM had a passion for writing and producing. He released few of his works even before debuting. He’s one of the top artists in Korea with the most songs credited to his name.

Namjoon’s 1st solo mixtape is titled RM with 11 tracks, all written by him - “Voice, Do You, Awakening, Monster, Throw Away, Joke, God Rap, Rush, Life, Adrift, I Believe”. His 2nd mixtape “Mono” was released in 2018 with 7 tracks - “tokyo, seoul, moonchild, badbye ft. eAeon, uhgood, everythingoes ft. Nell, and forever rain”. RM participated in writing all of the songs and helped produce 4 of them - “moonchild, badbye ft. eAeon, everythingoes ft. Nell, and forever rain”. Some tracks of this mixtape feature other artists in Korea - “badbye ft. eAeon, everythingoes ft. Nell”. RM has also produced and wrote songs for other artists such as - “GLAM, Warren G, MFBTY, Yankie, Primary, Homme, Gaeko, Wale, Honne, Beka, Younha, Tomorrow X Together, other BTS members’ solo songs, etc”.


Suga

Birth Name: Min Yoongi

On March 9, 1993, Min Yoongi was born in Daegu, South Korea. He started making music at such a young age. They were poor and his parents didn’t support his passion for music. Yoongi used to sell his works at the streets, but didn’t get paid well and people will just steal his tracks. He used to choose between buying himself dinner or paying the bus fare home because he didn’t have enough money. Suga was an underground rapper before being a trainee. He went by the name Gloss, which is the meaning of his name Yoongi when translated to english. He auditioned for Big Hit and signed with the company in 2010 through Hit It. He’s the second official member of BTS. While he was training, he also worked part time as a delivery driver. Unfortunately, in 2012, Yoongi got into a vehicle accident which injured his left shoulder. He kept it as secret from the company as he was afraid that he’ll get kicked out from the line-up. He suffered for almost 8 years treating his shoulders, but the condition got worse leading to him deciding to get a surgery in November of 2020. Min Yoongi made his debut with the name Suga as the group’s lead rapper. Suga used to play basketball a lot as a “shooting guard”, where he got his stage name from the first syllables of these words.

Suga has released 2 mixtapes so far outside of BTS, by the name “Agust D”. Agust D spelled backwards is Suga DT which is his stage name and his hometown “Daegu Town”. His first mixtape is titled “Agust D” [2016] - “Intro: DT sugA ft. DJ Friz, Agust D, give it to me, skit, 724148, 140503 at Agust dawn, The Last, Tony Montana ft. Yankie, Interlude: Dream, Reality, so far away ft. Suran” - and the 2nd titled “D-2” [2020] - “Moonlight, Daechwita, What Do You Think?, Strange ft. RM, 28 ft. Niihwa, Burn It ft. MAX, People, Honsool, Interlude: Set Me Free, Dear My Friend ft. Kim Jong-wan”. Both mixtapes have 10 tracks. For Agust D, all 10 tracks were written by him with other composers, 7 tracks - “Moonlight, Daechwita, Burn It ft. MAX, People, Honsool, Interlude: Set Me Free, Dear My Friend ft. Kim Jong-wan“ - are produced by him with the help of other producers. Four tracks of “Agust D” was fully written and produced by Suga - “Agust D, give it to me, skit, 724148” - while the other 6 were made with the help of other writters and producers - “Intro: DT sugA ft. DJ Friz, 140503 at Agust dawn, The Last, Tony Montana ft. Yankie, Interlude: Dream, Reality, so far away ft. Suran” -. These mixtapes also feature other artists - “RM, Niihwa, MAX, Kim Jong-wan, DJ Friz, Yankie, Suran”. He also made songs for other artists such as - “MAX, Juice Wrld, Coldplay, Jason Derulo, Lee So-ra, Halsey, Heize, OMI, other BTS members, etc.”.


J-hope

Birth Name: Jung Hoseok

Jung Hoseok was born in Gwangju, South Korea on February 18, 1994. Before becoming a trainee, Hoseok already had a passion in dancing and was an underground dancer. He auditioned at Big Hit Music in 2010 and made it as the 3rd official member part of the line up. By the stage name J-hope, he debuted as the group’s main dancer, lead rapper, and sub-vocalist. He is also part of BTS’s dance line.

In 2018, J-hope release his mixtape “Hope World” - “Hope World, P.O.P pt 1, Daydream, Base Line, Hangsang ft. Supreme Boi, Airplane, Outro: Blue Side”. He composed all of the 7 tracks and produced 3 tracks - “P.O.P pt 1, Base Line, Airplane” - with the help of other composers and producers. The track Hangsang featured Supreme Boi, a producer and song writer from the same company. He helped in making songs for the following artists: Jo Kwon, and other members of BTS.
