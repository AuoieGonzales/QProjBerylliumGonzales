The South-Korean Septet: BTS

BTS: We are Bulletproof

“If we can help you with your dream and your life a bit by our existence, our music, our performance, our pictures or videos, even if its not much. If we could reduce your pain from 100 to 99, 98 or 97, that makes our existence worthy.”, is a remark by Kim Namjoon better known as RM, the leader of the septet BTS at their ‘2017 BTS Trilogy III The Wings Tour The Final concert in Seoul.’ BTS is a 7-member full Korean boy group based in South Korea, under Bighit Music, formerly Bighit Entertainment. The name BTS is an abbreviation of the Korean term "Bangtan Sonyeondan" which translates to "Bulletproof Boy Scouts". The meaning of their name as explained on many interviews is "to block out stereotypes, criticisms, and expectations that aim on adolescents like bullets". BTS has a loyal fan base called the ARMYs. ARMY is an abbreviation for Adorable Representative MC for Youth. As ARMY can also mean people in military,the meaning behind it is that because BTS is bulletproof, they will always protect their fans and that they will always be together. Their fans support them by helping them break records, voting for them on award shows, trending them on social media accounts, buying their physical albums, organizing mass streaming events to boost their views and digital sales, and a whole lot more.

The group made their debut on June 13, 2013 releasing their first album "2 cool 4 skool". The title track of this album is the song titled “No More Dreams”. The group consists of 7 members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-hope, Jimin, V, Jungkook. Four members of the group are vocalists, while the other 3 are rappers. All of them participate in writing and producing their songs.To know more about their journey, check "Rise of Bangtan"


BTS MEMBERS

Rap Line

  • RM - RM is the leader and the 1st confirmed member of the group. He was born in Ilsan, South Korea and his full name is Kim Namjoon.
  • Suga - Born in Daegu, South Korea, the second eldest of the group is Min Yoongi. He has two stage names. He uses Suga for BTS and Agust D for his solo mixtapes.
  • J-Hope - Jung Hoseok, going by the stage name J-Hope, is the main dancer of the group. He was born in Gwangju, South Korea.

Vocal Line

  • Jin - Kim Seokjin, better known by his stage name Jin, is the vocalist and visual of the group. Born in Gwacheon, South Korea, he is the oldest member of the group.
  • Jimin - Park Jimin is the eldest of BTS’s maknae line (youngest line). He was born in Busan, South Korea and is also a main dancer of the group.
  • V - V is the stage name of the member Kim Taehyung. He was born in Busan, South Korea and grew up in Geochang. He’s also an actor and one of the casts of the K-drama “Hwarang”.
  • Jungkook - Born in Busan,South Korea, is the youngest member of the group. He’s called the Golden Maknae as he can rap, sing, dance, and is good at a lot of things.
