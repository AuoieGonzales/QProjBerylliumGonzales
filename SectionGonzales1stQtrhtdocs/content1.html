2013-2014

Coming from different hometowns, backgrounds and ages, Bighit debuted the seven with their first album “2 Cool 4 Skool”in 2013. Within these years, they released their first extended play “O!RUL8,2?”, a Japanese Album and their first studio album, “Dark & Wild”. From a company that almost got bankrupt, they all lived in a small apartment sharing one room. Each of the members worked very hard and is involved in writing and producing their songs. They started as a group with only a few fans. They used their managers’ personal stuff for props in their music videos to save money. They practiced for 16 hours a day up until their bodies give up. They won a lot of “new artist awards” from different shows, but they weren’t promoted well, since they came from a small unknown company. Since the start, there were a lot of people who didn’t believe in them, saying that it’s impossible for them to survive in the industry. Belittling them for wearing make up as it’s not acceptable to be called "hiphop". Music shows cut their performances and reduced their screen time. This is why BTS used social media to make themselves known by those who weren’t aware that their group exists. They also used these platforms to connect with their fans and promote their music. They uploaded pictures, their blogs, and dance practice videos. They also created these accounts prior to their debut to gain attention, posting a diary-like video of the members. This technique helped them reach a wide range of audience and gain fans abroad.


2015

Two years after debut, their hard work finally started to pay off. This year came the biggest turning point in their career. They tried a different music style. This earned them their first music show win on SBS “The Show” on May 5, 2015 with their song “I Need U’ in their album “The Most Beautiful Moment in Life”, followed by many more songs connected in a story line. Some of the lyrics of their songs also include problems of the society, opening the eyes of many to the sensitive topics of depression, mental health, inequalities, and a lot more which most artists in Korea do not dare to discuss about. BTS won against a big group, so people were suspicious and accused them of sajaegi. Sajaegi is when the company buying their own album to boost the release causing them to win this award. This was debunked when the Hanteo chart said that the sales were imputted all at once causing a sudden spike. Another thing is that the company they are in is pretty small and doesn’t have enough money to be capable of sajaegi.

2016: First Daesang

On 2016, the members promoted on Korean variety shows. They were mistreated by the hosts of most of these variety programs. The fans called May 2016 the terror month because of the series of accusations and attacks thrown at the group. They were also suddenly cut from the Busan One Asia Festival’s broadcast. Even though that’s the case, they still performed without cameras and proper lighting for their fans who waited for them. At the last parts of the year happened another big turning point. On November 19, 2016, the group won their 1st Daesang on Melon Music Awards (MMA). Daesang is the highest achievement award a group can receive. Their 1st daesang is the Album of the Year with “The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever”. December of the same year, the group won another daesang for the Artist of the year award on Mnet Asian Music Awards (MAMA). Coming from a small company, a lot were shocked by this win and accused the show of miscalculations.


2017

They released “You Never Walk Alone” on 2017, a repackaged album of the Wings album they released on 2016. The album broke records on pre-order and with the title track “Spring Day”. This song never left the chart since releasethat’s why it is called “Queen Spring Day” by the fans. It touched the hearts of many as the song and the music video is referenced to the “Sewol Ferry Tragedy”. That after the dark cold winter, the longing and pain, the spring, symbolizing hope will come.”Spring Day” also won best song of the year on MMA. 2017 is the year when BTS gained more fans attending their concert. The venues were bigger and the seats were full. They were breaking records after records. BTS also attended the Billboard Music Award (BBMAs) and won the “Top Social Artist” award. This is also the year when their “Love Yourself” series and the Love Myself Campaign with UNICEF was launched. The septet also collaborated with other artists, such as Andrew Taggart of Chainsmokers ans Steve Aoki. Their song DNA was also released on this year which charted in Billboard Hot 100. DNA also became their 1st song to reach 1 billion views in Youtube. BTS also performed at the AMAs or the America Music Awards. Lastly, they won major awards from the end of the year award shows, Golden Disk Awards (GDA) and Seoul Music Awards (SMA), becoming the 1st group not coming from the so called “big 3” companies to do so.

2018

In 2018, the group attended the BBMAs and performed “Fake Love”. They won the “Top Social Artist” award for the second time this year. The song also charted at number 10 on BB Hot 100. The group also attended the United Nations 2018 and their leader, RM delivered his speech promoting “Speak Yourself”. This same year, the group faced a lot of backlash for a shirt a member wore in 2017 and a Japanese show cancelled their performance. The fans were quick to defend the group and made a website named White Paper Project containing research works about the controversy. The company also responded and everything was cleared up at the end. BTS received the Order of Cultural Merit from the President of South Korea this year. This is given to the people that promotes the culture and helped with the countries development. The members are the youngest people to receive this kind of award only 5 years after debut. The group also charted on the Top 10 Album Chart.

2019

BTS attended the Grammy Awards this year. They also surpassed 5 billion views in Spotify. They also release their EP Map of the Soul: Persona with its title track Boy with Luv featuring Halsey. This album became the best-selling album in South Korea and “Boy With Luv” charted at number 8 on BB Hot 100. They also sold out the tickets for their concert at the Wembley Stadium. The group also won the “Favorite Duo or Group - Pop/Rock” and “Favorite Social Artist” awards at the AMAs. The group charted again on the Top 10 Album Chart.


2020

The Kpop group performed for the first time in the Grammys together with Lil Nas X January of this year. They performed “Old Town Road” since their leader made a remix with Lil Nas X titled “Seoul Town Road”. The group also beat their previous record of having the best-selling album “Map of the Soul: Persona” with their new release,”Map of the Soul 7”. The title track of this album, “ON”, charted number 4 at the BB Hot 100 being the highest charting song of theirs on the said chart. The album also got the number 1 spot for the 4th time on Billboard 200, being the only group to do so after “The Beatles”. They were suppossed to hold a tour, but got cancelled due to Covid-19. The group brodcasted on youtube their 4 pasts concerts for free for each day of the 2-day concert.

This is the year of their 1st full english song Dynamite. They released the song prior to the album as the members said that they had fun recording the song and wanted to share it as soon as possible. This is to cheer up the people, especially those having a hard time, during this pandemic. Dynamite became the 1st song of the group to debut and reach number 1 on Billboard Hot 100. The song stayed in top 10 for a total of 13 weeks. Dynamite also charted on many music platforms and was played in different radio stations. It also became the most viewed music video for the 1st 24 hours after release with more than 101.1 million views. The group also made their MTV Video Music Awards (VMAs) debut with the same song. They held the online concert named “Bang Bang Con: The Live” on their debut anniversary setting the record for the most viewers of a paid concert held online. BTS also released a remix of the song “Savage Love” with Jason Derulo. The song also charted at number 1 in Billboard Hot 100. BTS had their online concert titled Map of the Soul ON:E beating their previous record. The group released their album “BE” with its title track “Life Goes On”. The track debuted again as number 1 on the BB Hot 100 chart becoming the 1st song in Korean to reach that rank. BTS was also nominated in Grammys with “Dymanite” for “Best Pop Duo/Group Performance”

2021

The group performed “Dynamite” at the Grammys. They held another live stream of their 3 old concerts for free on Youtube. The group released another full english song titled “Butter” that broke their record in Dynamite. The music video garnered 108.2 million views 24 hours after its release. The song debuted as number one on the BB Hot 100. It stayed number in number 1 for 7 consecutive weeks and 10 weeks in total. The song that broke the consecutive weeks is their new release “Permission to Dance” that debut at number 1. The group met the President in the Blue House and he personally gave them 7 diplomatic passports as they’re going to have a speech and performance on the UN General Assembly (UNGA) 2021. BTS also appeared in Coldplay’s single “My Universe” which also debuted at number 1 on the same chart. The group is said to have an offline concert in the So-fi Stadium on November 27 and 28, and on December 1 and 2. The tickets for all four days are already sold out and this is gonna be their first concert with the fans since the pandemic started.

Conclusion:

BTS became popular worldwide, because they inspire others through their music in this digital world. They also helped a lot of their fans and led them to the way to loving and appreciating themselves, little by little. Not only that, but their awesome, well-made stages, great songs, touching lyrics, great team work, and fiery performances also attracted the attention of many from different age group, sexuality, and walks of life. They've encountered a lot of problems and obstacles , but were able to surpass them all together. They started at the bottom and worked hard on their way up. Despite the popularity the boys have stayed humble, true to themselves, and thankful to their fans from the beginning up to now. This is evident to the lyrics from their song “We Are Bulletproof: The Eternal” which is dedicated to their fans, “Even if I’m blocked off, I will still walk/We are, we are forever bulletproof/Yeah we are not seven, with you.”
